SnapCapsule

Taking throwaway portraits taken by users and turning them into pieces of art

Created by Mark Manalaysay and Travis Grant

Ever wish your Snapchats were more permanent?

I know, we don't think so as well.

How to do it

Send us a snap to @snapcapsule and we will screenshot it

Download on Github

Next, our resident painter will take your snap and turn it into an painting!

Exact dimensions are around 10'x10'

It'll then be added to our gallery of portraits, which will be shown at the Gustein Gallery at the end of the year

If you like the painting though, you could purchase it for the low, low price of $24.99
